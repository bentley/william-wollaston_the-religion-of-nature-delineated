Editor’s note: Wollaston did not translate his Greek, Latin, Hebrew, and Arabic quotations. John Clark did this (see the translator’s note). Wollaston also usually gave very brief, abbreviated sources for the quotations. In many cases I have been able to track these down and expand them (thanks in part to Alexander Altmann, who identified many of the Hebrew sources in a 1948 paper for the Jewish Historical Society of England) in the hopes of making these notes more useful.

I do not read any of those languages, so it’s very likely that I have let some typos slip into these notes. I apologize and invite you to report any errors you find at the Standard Ebooks group so that we can improve this edition. ―David Gross
